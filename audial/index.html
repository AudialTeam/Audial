Concept and Abstract

Tagline: Where All of your Audio Lives

Audial is an application built to allow you to organize your music tastes from all corners of the net. Right now Soundcloud and Spotify are to be supported. Potentially last.fm as well. Stretch goals are: to implement the ability to follow/like the artists and songs found using this tool through the program in which they are listed in your own account; to add the functionality of playing your liked music artists or songs in a custom playlist and being able to shuffle them and loop them / skip ahead or go back etc.
